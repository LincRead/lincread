Enki

An educational online game for children 9-12 for class room and home use.

Enki is a 2D platformer world inhabitated by creatures named Microlits, who used to rely on technology for everyday tasks. All their cloud-based services collapsed, so now they need help learning basic Maths, English and Natural Sciences from children.

Enki is unique because it's a web browser MMO with avatar costumization, crafting and leveling up, made specifically for schools. The game is played while the teacher follows his or her class' progress through a web-based dashboard. The teacher can even join in, give feedback on certain assignments ,and interact with his or her pupils through chat.
