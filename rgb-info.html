RGB

Destroy red, green and blue alien enemies using missiles with the same color.

This was originally our contribution to The Gathering Game Dev compo. It didn't win anything, but then the contribution was unknowingly automatically submitted to Norwegian Game Awards too. At that event it took the grand prize.
