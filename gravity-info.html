Gravity

Exploit gravity with your limited amount of fuel to complete mission objectives.

This was a family project for Norwegian Championship of Gameplay 2012. While I did the implementation my brother did the art work with MS Paint, and our dad did voice.

I got permission to use music from the Norwegian artist Philter.
