N.E.F Nevronus

Control a shrinked professor's shuttle in a brain with symptoms of epilepsy to learn more about them.

Our team won an idea contest for The Norwegian Epilepsy Organisation. The prize was funding to develop a web-based game that debunks myths about epilepsy in a fun and engaging way. Later we also released it as an app for mobile devices.

Year
2014
Framework
ImpactJS, HTML5
Development Time
2 months
Team size
5

Valuable experience and lessons

  • Use CocoonJS to port an HTML5 game to Android and iOS
  • Publish app on Android and iOS
  • Scaling an HTML5 game to a wide array of screen sizes and shapes
  • Implement a high-quality game with a very tight budget

The app is available on Google Play™ in Norway, Sweden and Denmark.
