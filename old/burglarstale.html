A Burglar's Tale

Sneak past goblin hordes and trolls in this 2D platformer.

The game idea was inspired by the scenes of Moria Mines in Lord of the Rings: Fellowship of the Ring. Sleeping goblins react on sound and wake each other up in a chain reaction.
