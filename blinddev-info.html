Blind Development

What happens when a programmer, artist, level designer, game designer and musician makes a game together, but have no clue what anyone else are doing prior to 1 hour before deadline? That's what we wanted to find out at Global Game Jam.

Year
2014
Framework
ImpactJS, HTML5
Development Time
48 hours
Team size
5

Valuable experience and lessons

  • If team members have full creative control over what they do, as long as they follow rules agreed upon, all the pieces might somehow fit together in the end
  • Full individual creativity from all members can increase the chance of creating something unique
