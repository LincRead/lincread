RTS Dev

Attempt to develop an RTS game with Unity.

I've dreamed about developing an RTS game since I was 13. It's what has driven me to learn programming ever since.

I tried to develop a 3D RTS game to see what I could achieve and not. I managed to use a third party A* pathfinding asset. In my application you could order your units to construct buildings, select and move multiple units, and make them attack other units. I was really obsessed with this project, but life circumstances eventually led me to leave the project. I'm still eager to do another attempt at an RTS one day.

Year

Framework

Valuable experience and lessons

  • Become familiar with a widely used 3D game engine
  • Discover that RTS games are difficult to develop and that I have to keep learning
  • Getting a more realistic perspective on scoping
  • Third party tools don't always apply well to your game concept
  • Blogging about my progress
