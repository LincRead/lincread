BoOooo

Scare humans away from your haunted mansions.

The prototype was a finalist at Norwegian Championship in Gameplay. Since team-work and result was successful the team decided to start up a company to continue development together. Everyone on the team had other work, so this was done on the side.

Several members of the team decided to start up Process Games.

Year

Framework
Game Maker

Team size
7
Honours and Awards
Nominee Best Innovation at Spillprisen 2015
Nominee Best Sound at Spillprisen 2015
Finalist Norwegian Championship in Gameplay 2014

Valuable experience and lessons

  • Start up game developer company
  • Develop a game with GameMaker
  • Publish a Premium Game on Android
  • Develop a high-quality mobile game with a very tight budget
  • Most of the work was done in our spare time, so it was very important to scope well
  • The prototype was played with mouse and keyboard. The biggest challenge was therefore to design controls and interface for mobile devices. We had to change the game design to accomplish this.

The app is available on Google Play™.
