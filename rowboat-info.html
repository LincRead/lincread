Rowboat Simulator

Software for Oslo Fjord Museum.

This was one of the most rewarding projects I've done.

I did this with my talented childhood friend and movie director Anders Øvergaard. To finish the project before launch we had to sleep over at the museum. I also had to co-operate with another friend of mine who is a hardware engineer.

Fun story: politicians and press were having their welcome drinks at the pre-opening of the museum, while I was still trying to fix the last known bug. I thought I fixed it after a restart, and the event was successful. Later I figured out it was a memory leak causing the occasional crashes. This is the grand attraction at the museum, so it was important that it worked properly. After a couple of months I heard that our attraction was the only one that didn't have bugs or other short-coming (they hired other firms to do other attractions), and that they were very pleased with our work.
